Central Week of Giving

The CUcare Week of Giving is a special time dedicated to supporting brilliant but financially disadvantaged students. At Central University we believe that education should be accessible to all, regardless of financial circumstances.

Contributions to the Fund are welcome from institutions, individuals, families and friends of CU.

Welcome To CU Day Of Giving

Official Launch of the CU Day of Giving by the Chancellor, Rev. Dr. Mesah Otabil.

Our program's goal is to help raise funds to support the financial needs of students in our school. We aim to gather an amount of money that will assist students in paying their fees, ensuring they can access quality education without financial barriers.
